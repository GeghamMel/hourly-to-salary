Minimum Wage Guide — Rates by Country 2024

8 min read · Reference · 2024

Minimum wage laws set the legal floor for worker compensation. Understanding minimum wage rates is important for employers, employees, and anyone comparing compensation across markets.

Living wage vs minimum wage

The living wage — the amount needed to cover basic living costs — is typically 2–3× the minimum wage in expensive cities. MIT's Living Wage Calculator estimates the living wage for a single adult in the US averages around $22–$25/hour, significantly above the $7.25 federal minimum.
