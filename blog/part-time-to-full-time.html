Part-Time to Full-Time Salary Guide

8 min read · Guide · 2024

Whether you're considering switching from part-time to full-time work, or trying to understand the income potential of your hourly rate at different hours, this guide covers everything you need.

Part-time to full-time conversion formula

Full-time equivalent annual salary = Hourly rate × 40 hours/week × 52 weeks = Hourly rate × 2,080

Or if you know your part-time annual income: Full-time equivalent = (Part-time annual income ÷ Part-time hours) × 2,080

What changes when you go full-time

  • Benefits eligibility: Most employers require 30+ hours for benefits
  • Tax bracket: Higher income may push you into a higher tax bracket
  • Retirement contributions: More income means more retirement savings potential
  • Work-life balance: Consider the lifestyle impact alongside the financial gain

Is full-time always financially better?

Not always. Consider:

  • Childcare costs that part-time allows you to avoid
  • Commute costs for additional days
  • Potential loss of flexibility that enables side income
  • Tax implications — some people find their effective tax rate increases significantly at full-time income levels
