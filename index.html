Frequently asked questions

Is this calculator accurate?
Yes. It uses standard salary conversion formulas and assumes consistent working hours.

Does this include taxes?
No. This calculator shows gross income before taxes.

Why is monthly salary different each month?
Monthly salary is calculated by dividing yearly income by 12 for simplicity.
